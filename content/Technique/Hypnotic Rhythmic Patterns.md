## Tresillo

- `/16 ~ ~ /16 ~ ~ /16 ~ |`
- 8 step sequence
- hypnotic
- **use velocity to make more interesting**

## Clave / Son clave

- 16 step sequence
- hypnotic

## Rumba clave

- `/16 ~ ~ /16 ~ ~ ~ /16 ~ ~ /16 ~ /16 ~ ~ ~ |`
- 16 step sequence
- hypnotic

## 12/8 Bell

- `x ~ x ~ x x ~ x ~ x ~ x |
- 12 step sequence
- example sounds: rims, clicks, rides, etc
- polymeter
- hypnotic
- tribal

## Shamanic Ride 1/8

- hits on every quarter note!
- accents: `x - - x - - - x | - - x - x - - -|`
- 2 bars!
- can do same and get Shamanic Ride 1/4 on quarter notes

## Kick and Toms

- kick `4x4`
- tom `-x-x--x--x-x--x-`
- using delays to create new rhythms
  - utility mono 200 Hz end of chain
  - sidechain ~60%
  - EQ dark or bright
