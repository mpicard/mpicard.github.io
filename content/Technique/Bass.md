## Bass

- 3 short stab-y quarter notes + 1 longer half or full note +2/3 semitones
- offbeats
  - works well make sure there is syncopated drums to offset the robotic-ness
- 16ths
  - every 2nd step reduce velocity
  - driving/peak time
  - alt 1: make second last 16th +1oct for **hypnotic** feel
  - alt 2: 2x 16ths 2x 16ths +1oct heavy side chain
