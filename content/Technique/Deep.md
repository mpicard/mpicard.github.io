In the style of Pambouk, Hraach, Armen Miran, Pablo Bolivar, etc on Hoomidaas
and Seven Villas

## Bouncy Dub

- bass 16ths pushed back almost by 32nd to give it lots of swing

## Pambouk Remix Dark Side

- breakdown
  - tension arp synth on time mode
  - filter goes up and down a few times before drop
  - high pass
- synths
  - call / tension arp becomes stab after drop
  - response / brassy synth arp
  - high string really quiet
- bass
  - minimal bassline with 2 notes
- drums
  - deep kick
  - tambourine openhat
- vocals fill up the track

## Pambouk & Armen Miran - Aware

- melodic techno lead brassy
- sub fills up low-end between kicks
- simple bassline
- in cooldown and breakdown
  - chord progression takes focus
  - lead fitlered down and in a simple arp

## Nhar - Easing Light

- simple core drums only
  - snare fills doing a lot to keep it interesting
  - simple hihats
- dubby chords
