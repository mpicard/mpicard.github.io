## Percussion & Groove

- 110 BPM
- lots of energy for a slow song
- 4x4 kick with snare on the 2&4
- pulse:
  - kick + snare
- syncopation:
  - woodblock
- low groove:
  - kick + bassline
  - low toms on 3/ 4/ + more on turnaround
- high groove:
  - perc motif
  - adds another perc on top before drop
  - tambourine shaker offbeat
  - mid/high toms reenforcing motif (only roots)
  - open hats slow feeling
