## Kick

- warm
- soft
- dirty

## Percussions

- polyrhythms with African drums
  - djembe, bata, congas, etc
  - also see Geometry of Rhythm
- percussions layered on top of bassline
- add a little swing to percussions
- dont swing hihats
  - put it on shakers
- when using loops, use clip mod to mute parts
- snare fills with OTT cut through mix
- NO CLAPS! 2/4 claps, usually more rhythmic claps

## Melodic

- jazz / soul influences for chords
- drone/pad throughout track that modulates and moves
- lots of melodic elements are percussive also
