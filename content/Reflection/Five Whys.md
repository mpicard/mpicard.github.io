- Why am I not finishing tracks?
  - perfectionism trap
- Why does it need to be perfect?
  - creative phase is safer, no judgement
- Why am I avoiding judging?
  - cannot hide behind process
  - in creative you can say "i'm still exploring"
  - in arrangement you can say "i'm still building"
  - but in final listen you have to answer "is this actually good?"
- Why am I hiding behind the process?
  - hard to judge emotional impact? expectations that it has a big emotional
    impact
- Why do I keep finding more things to fix instead of calling it done?
  - you are repeating the final listen instead of doing it once and done
- Why do I fear my work is not good enough?
  - emotional impact trap
  - [[Musical Identity - Conduit]] sets an incredibly high bar
    - transformative experience is beautiful but terrifying
  - _If work doesn't achieve goal, it threatens my musical identity_
  - detail-oriented producers often get lost and don't finish
  - impossible standards
