## Percussion & Groove

- 127 BPM
- classic house drums
- HPF kick+bass automation turn around up+down
  (https://youtu.be/2U32OLxjKVw?t=331)
  - add HH layer for top
- accent every 3rd hihat + swing
- C/L/R layered claps
  - tight swingy
- jacking hihats
  - tight skippy groove
  - ghost hat: layer that is 1/16th after offbeat pitched down 1st lower
    velocity
  - swing 60
  - every second hat needs a subtle perc layer

## Chords

- use m7, add7, etc jazzy

## Synths

- warm lush
- Rhodes / piano chords

- pads
  - with m7 chord jazzy (or find 2 notes besides each other in key)
